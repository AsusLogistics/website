HOW DO I KNOW IF THE DELIVERY ADDRESS AND COLLECTION ADDRESS IS WITHIN 100 MILES OF MIDLANDS?

CHECK ON GOOGLE MAPS BY TYPING THE COLLECTION POSTCODE AND THEN MIDLANDS AND LOOKING AT HOW MANY MILES IT SAYS ON GOOGLE MAPS. REPEAT WITH DELIVERY POSTCODE. IF BOTH POST CODES ARE WITHIN 100 MILES OF MIDLANDS THEN YOU SHOULD SELECT THE LOCAL DELIVERY OPTION

WHAT IS A SMALL ITEM?

ANY ITEM WHICH IS LESS THAN 1m x 0.5m x 0.5m in dimensions and weighs less than 50 kilos.

WHAT IS A LARGE ITEM?

ANY ITEM WHICH IS LESS THAN 1.6m x 0.8m x 0.8m in dimensions and weighs less than 80 kilos
