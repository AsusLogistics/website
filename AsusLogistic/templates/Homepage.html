About us


After working for several years for leading transport companies, it was clear that many avoidable incidents occurred from an impersonal and automated system. Missed deliveries, damaged parcels and poor customer service are a few of many issues that occur. In an increasingly fast paced world, these problems have knock on effects that leading companies fail to account for. At Asus Logistics, we prioritise creating a hassle-free experience to prevent this for our customers. How It WorksTheres only 4 keys steps!

  • Step 1: Description of Item

    a. What is the item? (e.g. sofa, table...)

    b. Tell us the dimensions? (e.g. cm, m, ft ...)

  • Step 2: Collection of Item

    c. We send a date & time frame to the seller

    d. Seller accepts or declines if not convenient

    e. The item is prepared for transportation

  • Step 3: Delivery of Item
